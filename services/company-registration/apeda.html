APEDA Registration

Export agricultural and processed food products from India. Get your RCMC from APEDA with expert assistance.

Apply Now

Description

APEDA (Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) registration is mandatory for exporters of scheduled products. It provides financial assistance, information, and guidelines for the development of scheduled products for export.

Documents Required

  • Import Export Code (IEC)
  • PAN Card of the Business
  • Bank Certificate
  • MoA/AoA or Partnership Deed

Process

1. IEC Issuance > 2. Online Application on APEDA Portal > 3. Document Verification > 4. Issuance of RCMC

Why DNT?

Expertise in food export regulations, seamless RCMC processing, and post-registration compliance support.

FAQ

Is IEC mandatory for APEDA? Yes, a valid Import Export Code (IEC) is mandatory for APEDA registration.
