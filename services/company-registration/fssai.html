Overview

FSSAI License Description

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) registration is mandatory for any individual or entity involved in food-related activities, including manufacturing, processing, storage, distribution, and sale of food products.

It ensures that the food products undergo quality checks, thereby reducing the chances of food adulteration and improving the accountability of food business operators (FBOs).

Types of FSSAI Registration

The license required depends on the turnover and scale of your business.

Basic Registration

For small businesses or startups with an annual turnover below ₹12 Lakhs.

State License

For medium-sized businesses with an annual turnover between ₹12 Lakhs and ₹20 Crores.

Central License

For large businesses with a turnover exceeding ₹20 Crores or those operating in multiple states.
