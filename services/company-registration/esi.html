Understanding ESI Scheme

ESI (Employee State Insurance) is a self-financing social security and health insurance scheme for Indian workers, providing medical and financial benefits to employees.

Medical Benefit

Full medical care is provided to an insured person and his family members from the day he enters insurable employment.

HEALTH

Maternity Benefit

Maternity benefit for confinement/pregnancy is payable for 26 weeks, which is extendable by a month on medical advice.

FAMILY

Disablement Benefit

Financial assistance provided to employees in case of temporary or permanent disablement arising out of employment injury.
