— OVERVIEW —

Why Go Public?

A Public Limited Company is the ultimate business structure for large-scale operations. It offers the highest level of transparency and allows for raising significant capital from the general public.

Massive Funding

Ability to raise significant capital from the general public through Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) and follow-on offers.

IPO READY

Brand Supremacy

Highest level of transparency and regulatory compliance leads to supreme market credibility and brand value.

ELITE STATUS

Free Transfer

Shares of a Public Limited company are freely transferable, offering the highest liquidity to all shareholders.

LIQUID EQUITY
DOCUMENTATION

Essential Documents

Detailed requirements for incorporating a Public Limited enterprise.

Board KYC

PAN and Aadhar card of minimum 3 directors and 7 shareholders.

Director DSC

Digital Signature Certificates for all designated directors.

Office Proof

Utility Bill and NOC from the owner for the registered office address.

MOA & AOA

Articles and Memorandum drafted for large-scale operations.

WORKFLOW

Incorporation Steps

01

DSC & DIN

Obtaining digital signatures and IDs for directors and founders.

02

Name Approval

Securing your corporate identity via the MCA RUN service.

03

Portal Filing

Submission of Spice+ forms with detailed MOA/AOA attachments.

04

COI Issued

Issuance of Certificate of Incorporation with PAN and TAN.
