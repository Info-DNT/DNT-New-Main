— OVERVIEW —

Why Choose Pvt Ltd?

Private Limited Company is the most popular corporate legal entity in India. It is governed by the Companies Act, 2013, and is designed for businesses aiming for high growth and structural stability.

Investor Magnet

Pvt Ltd companies are the only entities easily capable of issuing shares to raise venture capital and PE funding.

SCALE READY

Global Credibility

The "Pvt Ltd" suffix adds instant trust for vendors, banks, and international partners across the globe.

PREMIUM STATUS

Easy Transfer

Ownership can be easily transferred through the simple transfer of shares, allowing for easy exit or entry of partners.

DOCUMENTATION

Essential Documents

A curated list of requirements for a hassle-free registration process.

Directors' KYC

PAN and Aadhar card of minimum two directors.

DSC

Digital Signature Certificate for all proposed directors.

Office Proof

Utility Bill and NOC from the property owner for the office.

MOA & AOA

Customized Memorandum and Articles of Association.

WORKFLOW

Registration Process

01

DSC & DIN

Obtaining digital signatures and director IDs for all founders.

02

Name Approval

Reserving your brand name through the MCA's Spice+ Part A.

03

Portal Filing

Comprehensive submission of Spice+ Part B, AGILE, and INC-9.

04

COI Issued

Final certificate issuance with PAN, TAN, and DIN numbers.
