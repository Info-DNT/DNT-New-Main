— NON-PROFIT ENTITY —

Corporate Structure for Social Good

A Section 8 Company is preferred by modern NGOs for its formal structure, limited liability, and higher transparency which attracts corporate CSR funding.

Donation Benefits

Easier to obtain 80G/12A certificates, allowing donors to claim tax deductions on contributions.

FINANCIAL

Limited Liability

Protects personal assets of directors from company debts, unlike traditional trusts or societies.

LEGAL

Universal Credibility

Recognized globally by international donors and government agencies for its strict compliance.

DOCUMENTATION

NGO Requirements

Special documentation is needed for the MCA license (Section 8 License).

Financial Est.

Projected Income & Expenditure for the next 3 years.

Charitable MoA

Memorandum drafted strictly with charitable/social objects.

Director KYC

PAN, Aadhar, and Photos of at least 2 directors.

MILESTONES

Licensing Workflow

01

Digital IDs

Obtaining DSC and DIN for the founding board members.

02

Name Approval

Reservation of a non-profit name (e.g., Foundation, Association).

03

MCA License

Application for License under Section 8 (Form INC-12).

04

Incorporation

Final SPICe+ filing and Certificate of Incorporation issuance.
