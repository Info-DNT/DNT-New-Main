— WHY TRADEMARK? —

Protect Your Most Valuable Asset

A trademark is the unique identity of your business. Registering it ensures no one else can profit from your hard-earned reputation.

Legal Protection

Registering gives you the legal right to sue anyone who uses your brand without permission.

ENFORCEABLE

Intangible Asset

A trademark can be sold, licensed, or used as collateral, increasing your business valuation.

VALUE BUILDER

Brand Trust

The ® symbol adds instant credibility and professional trust in the global marketplace.

PREMIUM STATUS
DOCUMENTATION

Essential Requirements

A curated list of documents for a hassle-free registration process.

Logo / Brand Name

Soft copy of the logo or the name/slogan to be protected.

Identity Proof

PAN and Aadhar card of the applicant (Individual or Entity).

User Affidavit

Required if you claim usage before the application date.

MSME / Startup

Certificates to claim 50% discount on Govt. fees.
