Understanding CE Marking

The CE mark is a mandatory conformity marking for certain products sold within the European Economic Area (EEA). It indicates that a product has been evaluated by the manufacturer and is considered to meet EU safety, health and environmental protection requirements.

Electronics

Safety and electromagnetic compatibility for electronic devices.

POPULAR

Medical Devices

Strict health and safety compliance for medical equipment.

REQUIRED

Machinery

Operational safety standards for industrial and consumer tools.
