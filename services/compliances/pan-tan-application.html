PAN vs TAN: Which do you need?

Both are 10-digit alphanumeric numbers issued by the Income Tax Department, but they serve different purposes in the tax ecosystem.

PAN (Permanent)

Required for filing income tax returns, opening bank accounts, and high-value financial transactions.

IDENTITY

TAN (Deduction)

Mandatory for anyone responsible for deducting tax at source (TDS) or collecting tax at source (TCS).
